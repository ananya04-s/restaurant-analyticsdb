# 📌 Overview

Restaurant Analytics Dashboard is a comprehensive Business Intelligence solution developed using **Power BI** to help restaurant owners and managers analyze operational performance through interactive visualizations and real-time business metrics.

The dashboard transforms raw restaurant data into meaningful insights, enabling data-driven decisions related to sales, customer behavior, menu performance, profitability, and operational efficiency.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze restaurant sales performance
- Identify top-selling menu items
- Monitor customer ordering trends
- Track revenue and profitability
- Improve inventory planning
- Enhance customer satisfaction
- Support strategic business decisions
